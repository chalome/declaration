Steps for Declaration

Follow these simple steps to complete your tax declaration:

  1. Step 1: Authentication - Log in to your account. If you don't have an account, register to create one.
  2. Step 2: Fill Out the Declaration - Use the app to provide all the necessary details for your tax declaration.
  3. Step 3: Submit for Approval - Once completed, submit your declaration. The admin will review and approve it.
  4. Step 4: Receive Confirmation - After approval, you will receive an email confirmation from the admin.

Our platform ensures a secure and efficient process for your tax declarations.
